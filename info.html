ScanCoFight

ScanCoFight is a site to raise awareness of citizens toward Covid-19. This site is created to not just raise awareness, but also to give information about ways to prevent the virus from spreading throughout the community.

Covid-19 symptoms:

common: fever, dry cought, tiredness.
less-common: aches and pains, sore throat, diarhea, headache,rash.
serious: difficulty breathing, chest pain, lost of speech.

Way to prevent the spreading of Covid-19:

1. keep distance between people a least 1-2m.
2. wearing face mask when outing.
3. use hand sanitizer when you are touching things outside.
4. staying home.
5. minimize interaction with people face to face.
